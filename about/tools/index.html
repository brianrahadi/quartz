    Tools are helpful things I found to enjoy using. It’s a notable list of any software and hardware I use daily. My enjoyment of using software that helps people live better kinda pulled me into my current major.

    # Software

    Applications:

    • TickTick serves as my to-do list x habit tracker x pomodoro timer
    • Learning Obsidian to capture my thoughts
    • I also use Notion occassionally for its tables and collaboration features
    • Forest have helped me to be indistractable
    • one sec and Opal have also helped me tremendously in reducing my phone’s social media and screen time consumption
    • Spotify is a must have during commute or when writing cringy internet stuff like now.
    • Kindle as my go-to book reader app
    • Snipd is my podcast app as it allows saving annotations (highlights last 15 seconds)
    • Trying to consistently use Readwise to re-read highlights imported from my Kindle and Snipd.

    Chrome Extensions:

    • StayFocusd have helped me to block distracting websites during focus hours
    • Video Speed Controller mainly to watch lecture recordings in faster speed
    • Distraction Fee Youtube have made me navigate youtube with less temptations. It blocks the trending tabs, youtube shorts, and fully customizable.
    • News Feed Eradicator blocked most social media websites’ timeline so I won’t need to scroll the hot feeds.
    • Simplify is really useful when applying for jobs. It auto-fills common inputs in one click.

    # Hardware

    • I use the latest MacBook Pro with Intel as my daily driver
    • I also really enjoy using LG 34" Ultrawide Monitor as I can open 3 relatively large windows at the same time + my laptop screen.
    • I type and scroll with Logitech Keyboard and Mouse set. Definitely recommended!
    • The set is placed on a Logitech Studio Desk Mat for extra protection and aesthetics ;)
    • Sometimes, I am standing with my Ikea Trotten Standing Desk
    • If not, I am sitting on my Kerdom Office Chair
    • I try to make time to read books on my 4th Gen Kindle Paperwhite
